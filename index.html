About Me

Hello, my name is Hector Velásquez. I’m a proud Salvadoran, and I’ve been working in the handyman business since the year 2000. With over 24 years of experience, I’ve had the honor of serving homeowners and businesses across Washington D.C., Maryland, and Virginia and beyond.

I lead a hardworking team, and together we take pride in delivering high-quality results — from electrical work and plumbing to painting, cleaning, remodeling, and more. I love every part of what I do. Whether it's a small repair or a big project, I always give it my all.

My clients often come through referrals, because I believe that honesty, punctuality, and attention to detail are what make the difference. I started this journey with just my hands and a dream, and today, Velásquez Home Services is a reflection of my dedication and passion to help families improve their homes.
